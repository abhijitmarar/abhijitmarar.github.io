Abhijit Marar

PhD Student in Operations Management

Naveen Jindal School of Management, University of Texas at Dallas

I am a PhD student in Management Science with a concentration in Operations Management at the University of Texas at Dallas. My research interests lie in Empirical Market Design, Matching Markets, and Operations Management in Education. I am advised by Ignacio Rios.

Prior to joining UT Dallas, I obtained a PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Georgia, an MSc in Biomedical Engineering from Ruprecht-Karls Universität Heidelberg, and a BSc in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I also have industry experience in photonics and scientific instrumentation.
