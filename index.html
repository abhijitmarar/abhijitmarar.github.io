I am a PhD student in Management Science with a concentration in Operations Management at the University of Texas at Dallas. My research interests lie in Empirical Market Design, Matching Markets, and Operations Management in Education. I am advised by Prof. Ignacio Rios.

Prior to joining UT Dallas, I completed a PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Georgia, where my work focused on advanced optical microscopy and nanoscale imaging. I also hold an MSc in Biomedical Engineering from Ruprecht-Karls Universität Heidelberg and a BSc in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
