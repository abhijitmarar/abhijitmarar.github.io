I am a PhD student in Analytics at the Mendoza College of Business, University of Notre Dame. My research interests lie in Empirical Market Design, Matching Markets, and Operations Management in Education. I am advised by Prof. Ignacio Rios.

Prior to joining Notre Dame, I completed a PhD in Electrical Engineering at the University of Georgia, where my work focused on advanced optical microscopy and nanoscale imaging. I also hold an MSc in Biomedical Engineering from Ruprecht-Karls Universität Heidelberg and a BSc in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
